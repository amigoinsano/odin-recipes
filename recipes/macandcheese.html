Mac and Cheese

Description

A simple Mac and Cheese Recipe. Can be used as a side dish or on its own.

Ingredients

  • 230g elbow macaroni
  • 2tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar chees
  • 230g cuped processed cheese food

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni elbow in large pot with salted water
  2. Melt butter in a medium pan, whisk flour and stir. Add milk and cook until thick and bubbly (5 to 7 minutes) Add cheese and keep stirring until completely melted.
  3. Mix drained pasta and cheese sauce in a large bowl.
  4. Pour mixture in a greased casserole dish. Bake in preheated oven (175Â°C) for 30 minutes.

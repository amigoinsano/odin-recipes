Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups mik
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted

Steps

  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a large bowl. Make a hole in the center and pour in milk, egg and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat an oiled griddle or frying pan on medium heat. Pour batter onto griddle and bake pancakes.

Fake Rabbit

Description

A traditional German Meatloaf recipe called "Falscher Hase" - Fake Rabbit.

Ingredients

  • 600g ground meat (300g pork, 300g beef)
  • 1 1/2 dinner rolls
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 eggs for the loaf
  • 5 eggs for boiling
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • Dijon mustard (1tbsp)
  • Pepper, Salt
  • 14 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Soak dinner rolls in warm water
  2. Preheat oven to 170Â°C
  3. In a pot, bring water to boil, cook 5 eggs for about 7 minutes and peel them (after letting them cool down)
  4. Put ground meat, 2 eggs, mustard, onion, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  5. Chop parsley, press garlic and add them to the bowl.
  6. Squeeze water from soaked rolls and put them into the bowl as well.
  7. Mix everything to a smooth mass.
  8. Place some parchment paper on a baking sheet and put the bacon slices (overlapping) onto it in a large rectangle.
  9. Place the mixture on top of the bacon, leaving a large rim underneath.
  10. Make an indention in the center of the mixture and put the boiled eggs in this indention.
  11. Grab one side of the parchment and flip half the meat-mixture over the eggs. Repeat from the other side. Shape and wrap the loaf with bacon.
  12. Bake for an hour
